# Dictionary

Boss Monster: the main character the player controls. If defeated, the game is over.

Stage: simply a game stage that the player can choose to play.

Wave: a game stage consists of multiple waves. Each wave will generate a Hero Team. The player needs to defeat the team to pass the wave.

Preparation Phase: Before each wave, there is a short rest for the player to do some preparation, including shuffling the deck and redrawing cards.

Hero Team: A group of *Enemy Heroes* that come to challenge the player in each wave. A hero team typically consists of 6 - 10 Enemy Heroes.

Enemy Hero: An enemy unit of the player. Each may have a *Class*, some *Equipment* and some *Hero Skills*.

Class: The type (occupation) of an Enemy Hero. Examples are Warrior, Mage, Ranger, Knight, etc.

Equipment: An Enemy Hero may randomly equip some equipment. Equipment provides stats, and some may provide extra effects.

Hero Skill: An Enemy Hero may randomly equip some Hero Skills that have special effects. At least one basic attack skill is equipped according to the Hero Class.

Minion: A smaller monster that is summoned by the player side, either by cards or the Boss Monster’s effect.

Battle Field: The field where the battle takes place in a game.
