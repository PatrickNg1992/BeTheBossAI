# Card Effect Design

A card effect may consist of one or more of the following card effects.

Rush: Move the boss at high speed.

Teleport: Immediately transfer the target to another position.

Recover: Heal the Boss Monster or minions’ health.

Buff or Debuff: Apply effects to allies or enemies.

Attack: Do a special attack.

Interrupt: Interrupt the enemy's actions.

Draw Cards: Draw more cards.

Summon: Summon minions or obstacles on the battlefield.
