# Boss Monster

On the battlefield, the player controls a boss-level monster to defeat the enemy heroes. The boss monster should be chosen before the game from a list of player-owned boss monsters. 

## Battling

The Boss monster will fight the enemy heroes automatically, and each monster has its own AI and behaviour. 

The player can make two instructions to interact with battle: 

- choose a target enemy/location for the monster

- play a card from hand



## Target

When the game starts, the aim will target a random enemy. The player can click on the field to change the target. If you click on an enemy, it will be chosen, and the monster will try to attack the target enemy. If you click on the battlefield, the clicked position will be the target, and the monster will move towards it.



## Play a card

Player can play a card from his hand. Choose a card and click on the battle field to play its effect. After the card is played, it will goto discard pile. Player can click the card again to cancel the play.
